# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Card Upload
Capture notebook photos, extract words and phrases via OCR, validate with an LLM, and review or edit cards before adding them to a deck.

### 2. Deck Management
Create, edit, and organize flashcard decks by language level (B1.1, B1.2, etc.) or custom topic, and browse or manage cards within each deck.

### 3. Practice Session
Drill flashcards using spaced repetition with difficulty ratings, toggle between Spanish-first and English-first modes, and track session results.

### 4. Dashboard
View progress stats, mastery levels per deck and card, study streaks, and identify weak areas that need more practice.

### 5. Settings & Account
Manage practice preferences, direction defaults, PWA install, and optional authentication for future multi-user support.
